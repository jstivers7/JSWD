# Outcome-led copy — draft options

A reworking of the homepage hero and the three service tiers to lead with the *value the site creates* rather than the deliverable — while keeping your "right platform for the job" differentiator. Nothing here is live yet; pick what you like and I'll apply it.

**Option B — audience-specific outcomes**
> **Bay Area Web Designer**
> More clients. More donations. More readers.
> I design websites that do real work for small businesses, nonprofits, and authors — built to bring in inquiries, support, and sales, on the platform that fits you best.

**Option C — credibility + outcome (softer, safest on promises)**
> **Bay Area Web Designer**
> A site that makes people take you seriously — and take action.
> I help small businesses, nonprofits, and creatives show up polished and credible online, so the right people get in touch, give, or buy. Built on the platform that's right for your goals, not just the one I happen to use.

*Recommendation:* **C** fits your client mix (authors/nonprofits/arts) without over-promising metrics you don't control, while still being outcome-led. **B** is punchiest if you want to be bolder.

## Service tiers

The idea: keep the names, but each tier's one-liner names *who it's for and what it helps them achieve*, then the feature list stays as the "what's included." Pricing unchanged.

### Launch — *Get found and get inquiries, fast*
**Current "best for":** Solopreneurs & small businesses
**Outcome-led:** For solopreneurs and small businesses who need to look credible and start getting inquiries — a clean, professional site that turns a quick search into a phone call.
*Starting at $1,800 · ~1 week*

### Growth — *Built to bring in more*
**Current "best for":** Growing businesses & nonprofits
**Outcome-led:** For growing businesses and nonprofits that need their site to pull its weight — more pages, a blog, e-commerce or donations, and the structure to convert visitors into customers and supporters.
*Starting at $3,500 · 2–4 weeks*

### Custom — *Stand out and be remembered*
**Current "best for":** One-of-a-kind projects
**Outcome-led:** For organizations and creatives who need to make an impression — a fully custom, interactive site that sets you apart from everyone using the same template, and keeps people engaged.
*Price on request*
