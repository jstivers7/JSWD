About

A designer who picks the
right tool for the job.

Most freelancers know one platform and steer every client toward it. I work across six — so my advice starts with what's best for you, not what's easiest for me.

I believe every client has their own voice, personality, and purpose — and your website should reflect that. At Jeannie Stivers Web Design, I create custom websites designed specifically around your goals, your industry, and your audience.

With more than a decade of experience in web design and development, I've worked on everything from author websites to full e-commerce platforms. I also provide search engine optimization and keyword research to help your website reach the people it's meant to serve.

When specialized development is needed, I use modern AI-assisted development tools to make sure your site functions beautifully behind the scenes as well as on the surface.

I founded JSWD during the coronavirus pandemic in 2020, turning years of experience into a dedicated business focused on helping clients build meaningful and effective online presences.

What ties it together is range. Some projects are best served by a quick, self-manageable Squarespace site; others call for a design-forward Webflow build, a content-heavy WordPress site, a Shopify store, or a fully hand-coded static site. Because I'm comfortable across all of them, I can recommend the right fit honestly — so you're never paying for more than you need or boxed into a tool that doesn't suit you.
