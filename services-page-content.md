# Services

## The right platform, a polished site, none of the hassle.

I'm a web designer who helps small businesses, nonprofits, and creative professionals get online with a site that actually fits them. Because I build across six platforms — Squarespace, Webflow, WordPress, Shopify, GitHub Pages, and fully custom code — I'm not trying to sell you on one tool. I help you find the right one for your goals, your budget, and how hands-on you want to be after launch.

Most projects fall into one of three packages. If yours doesn't, that's what the custom work section is for.

---

## Packages

### Launch — for getting online, fast
**Best for:** solopreneurs, creatives, and small local businesses that need a clean, professional presence without a lot of moving parts.

- Up to 5 pages, designed and built on Squarespace (or a similar managed platform)
- Fully mobile-responsive
- Basic SEO setup so you can be found
- Contact form and social links
- One round of revisions

**Starting at $1,800** · Typical turnaround: about 1 week

---

### Growth — for businesses that need more
**Best for:** growing businesses and nonprofits that need more pages, more functionality, and room to expand.

- Custom design built on WordPress, Webflow, or Shopify — whichever fits your needs best
- More pages and a tailored layout
- Blog setup, contact forms, and basic e-commerce or donation integration
- Two rounds of revisions
- Guidance on managing the site yourself after launch

**Starting at $3,500** · Typical turnaround: 2–4 weeks

---

### Custom — for something one of a kind
**Best for:** organizations and brands that want a distinctive, fully custom site with interactive or animated elements.

- Fully custom design and development
- Animations, interactive features, and complex integrations
- Built on the platform that best serves the project (including hand-coded static sites)
- Examples: an animated financial dashboard for a nonprofit, a children's book author site with custom illustration-driven layouts

**Price on request** (starting at $7,500) · Timeline scoped to the project

## Add-ons

Bolt these onto any package, or book them on their own for an existing site:

- **SEO audit** ($300–$600) — find and fix what's holding your search ranking back
- **Site speed optimization** ($250–$500) — faster load times, better conversions
- **Accessibility review** ($400–$800) — make your site usable for everyone (and lower your legal risk)
- **Email template design** ($200–$500) — branded, responsive newsletters and campaigns
- **Monthly maintenance** ($75–$200/mo) — updates, backups, small content changes, and peace of mind

## How it works

1. **Discovery call** — we talk through your goals, audience, and what success looks like.
2. **Proposal & platform pick** — I recommend the right package and platform and give you a clear quote.
3. **Design & build** — I create and develop your site, sharing progress as we go.
4. **Review & revisions** — you give feedback; we refine until it's right.
5. **Launch & handoff** — your site goes live, and I show you how to manage it (or handle it for you on a maintenance plan).
